Why It Matters

Canada's Tax Code Is Complex — We Make It Work For You

The Canadian tax system is one of the most intricate in the world, with constantly shifting federal and provincial rules, income-tested credits, and complex corporate structures that interact in ways most taxpayers never fully understand. Reactive tax filing — submitting a return once a year with no advance strategy — is the single most expensive financial mistake Canadians make. The difference between a good tax plan and a great one can amount to tens of thousands of dollars every year.

At Key Metrics Accounting, we treat tax planning as a year-round discipline. Our team monitors legislative changes, proactively models scenarios, and coordinates across your personal, corporate, and investment structures to ensure every dollar of available savings is captured — before the filing deadline, not scrambling after it. Whether you're a business owner, investor, professional, or high-net-worth individual, we build a strategy calibrated precisely to your situation and goals.

  • Year-round tax monitoring and adjustment
  • RRSP/TFSA optimization timing
  • Corporate income splitting strategies
  • Multi-provincial compliance management
  • Capital gains and loss harvesting
  • HST/GST planning and registration

What We Cover

Comprehensive Tax Strategy Across Every Area of Your Financial Life

Our tax strategists work across every dimension of Canadian tax law — from corporate structures to personal investments — to deliver a cohesive plan that minimizes your total tax burden.

📊

Corporate Tax Minimization

Income splitting, holding companies, CCPC deductions, and dividend vs. salary optimization for business owners who want to legally extract wealth from their corporations at the lowest possible tax cost.

🏠

Real Estate Tax Planning

Principal residence exemptions, rental income strategies, and property transfer planning. We ensure your real estate portfolio is structured for maximum after-tax returns at every stage of ownership.

💼

Business Owner Strategies

Owner-manager compensation planning, RRSP contributions, individual pension plans (IPPs), and surplus stripping strategies designed specifically for incorporated professionals and entrepreneurs.

🌍

International Tax

Foreign income reporting (T1135), tax treaty planning, and cross-border transaction structuring for Canadians with international business interests, foreign assets, or non-resident income.

📈

Investment Tax Optimization

Capital gains timing, loss harvesting, prescribed rate loans for income splitting, and portfolio tax efficiency strategies that significantly reduce your annual investment tax bill.

🎯

SR&ED Tax Credits

Identify and claim Scientific Research & Experimental Development investment tax credits for qualifying businesses — one of the most valuable and underutilized programs in the Canadian tax code.
