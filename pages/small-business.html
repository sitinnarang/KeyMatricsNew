Cash Flow

Management of Bills, Payments & Cash

Cash flow is the lifeblood of every small business. Poor cash management is the number one reason small businesses fail — even profitable ones. When you don't know what's coming in, what's going out, and when, you make decisions in the dark.

Key Metrics Accounting gives you complete visibility into your cash position with real-time AP/AR tracking, 13-week cash flow forecasts, and monthly bank reconciliation. We help you understand not just where your money is, but where it will be — so you can invest, hire, and grow with confidence.

Even below that threshold, voluntary registration can be beneficial — it allows you to claim input tax credits on business purchases. We'll advise on the best approach for your situation.
