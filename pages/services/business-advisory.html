Overview

Beyond the Numbers — Financial Strategy That Drives Real Growth

Most accounting relationships are backward-looking: your accountant tells you what happened last year, files your return, and sends you an invoice. That's compliance accounting — necessary, but nowhere near enough if you're running a business with real ambitions. What separates growing companies from stagnant ones is usually the quality of their financial decision-making, and that requires a different kind of financial partner.

Key Metrics Accounting's Business Advisory practice brings CFO-level strategic thinking to Canadian SMEs through a fractional model — meaning you get the financial leadership your business needs without the $250,000 salary that typically comes with it. From cash flow forecasting and financial modeling to corporate restructuring and exit planning, we work alongside you to turn your financial data into your most powerful competitive advantage.

  • Fractional CFO & Controller services
  • Financial modeling and forecasting
  • Business valuation
  • Cash flow optimization
  • Corporate structure optimization
  • Exit planning and M&A support
