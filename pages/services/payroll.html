Overview

Your Employees Deserve to Be Paid Right — Every Single Time

Payroll is one of the most time-sensitive and legally consequential obligations a business owner faces. A missed CRA remittance triggers penalties of 3 to 10 percent plus interest — and that's before considering the trust damage a late paycheque causes with your team. Getting it right every cycle isn't optional; it's foundational to how your business operates and how your people feel about working there.

Multi-province payroll adds another layer of complexity that trips up even experienced HR departments. Each province has its own employment standards, statutory holiday rules, minimum wage rates, and tax brackets. Key Metrics Accounting manages all of it — from first payroll run to annual T4 filing — so your team gets paid accurately and on time, and CRA never has a reason to come knocking.

  • Weekly, bi-weekly, and semi-monthly payroll runs
  • Direct deposit and pay stub delivery
  • CRA remittances (CPP, EI, income tax)
  • T4 and T4A slip preparation
  • ROE (Record of Employment) filing
  • Multi-province payroll compliance
