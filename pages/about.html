Our Founding Story

From a Small Office to Industry Leaders

Founded in 2010 with a simple belief: every client deserves the same caliber of financial expertise previously only available to Fortune 500 companies.

FinancePro was founded by James Anderson and Sarah Mitchell in the aftermath of the 2008 financial crisis — a moment that made it abundantly clear how critical sound financial guidance was for businesses of all sizes. What began as a two-person tax practice in a small Manhattan office has grown into a premier full-service accounting and advisory firm with a team of over 35 specialists.

From our earliest days, we set ourselves apart by treating every client relationship as a long-term partnership rather than a transactional engagement. We take the time to deeply understand each client's business model, industry dynamics, and personal goals before recommending any course of action.

Today, FinancePro serves over 500 clients across 18 states, managing more than $2 billion in client assets and saving our clients an estimated $20 million in taxes annually. We've been recognized by Inc. Magazine, Accounting Today, and the American Institute of CPAs for our innovation, client service, and professional excellence.

Milestones

Our Journey Through the Years

Fifteen years of growth, milestones, and unwavering commitment to client success.

2010

Founded in New York

James Anderson and Sarah Mitchell founded FinancePro with 8 initial clients, two staff members, and a vision to democratize premium financial advisory services.

2013

Expanded to 50 Clients

Rapid growth through referrals led us to hire our first 10 employees and move to a larger office in the Financial District. Launched our bookkeeping and payroll divisions.

2015

Launched Business Advisory Practice

Hired Michael Chen as our first CFP and Elena Rodriguez as Tax Director. Launched our Business Advisory division, adding CFO-as-a-Service to our offerings.

2017

First Major Industry Award

Named "Fastest-Growing Accounting Firm" by Accounting Today magazine. Surpassed 200 clients and $500M in managed assets. Opened our second office in Boston.

2020

COVID-19 Crisis Response

Pivoted rapidly to remote operations and provided critical PPP loan guidance, CARES Act analysis, and emergency cash flow consulting to over 150 clients, saving many businesses from closure.

2022

Technology Transformation

Launched our proprietary client portal and integrated AI-powered financial analytics platform. Achieved SOC 2 Type II certification, underscoring our commitment to data security.

2025

$2B+ Assets Under Management

Crossed the $2 billion threshold in client assets managed. Expanded to 35+ professionals, 500+ clients across 18 states. Named one of Inc. 5000's fastest-growing companies.
